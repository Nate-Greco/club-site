What We Do

Our goal is to create a space where students can learn to code, build projects, and share ideas with each other. We focus on hands‑on learning, collaboration, and making technology accessible and fun. By running workshops, hackathons, and community projects, we aim to help members grow their skills and confidence while contributing to something bigger than themselves. Find us at weekly meetings and be sure to follow our socials for special events.
